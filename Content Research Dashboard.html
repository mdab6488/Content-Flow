Keyword Gap & YouTube Topic Validator
Uncover high-intent, profitable keywords your competitors are already monetizing. This tool identifies valuable cross-sell opportunities through keyword overlap and validates profitability using CPC data to estimate YouTube RPM and affiliate potential. By integrating search trends, it ensures you only invest in growing markets. It also pinpoints massive content gaps by highlighting low-competition topics (VidIQ score < 50) where high-traffic articles (>5K/mo) face little competition from top YouTube videos (<100K views).
🎯 Sweet Spot Filter: 1K–50K Traffic + Intent = Commercial (C) or Informational (I). These pages rank well enough to validate the niche but don't have the authority to defend against a superior piece of content.
💡 PRO TIP: The CPC Predictor
CPC above $1.50 = high buyer intent. CPC above $3.00 = very lucrative niche with excellent YouTube RPM & affiliate conversion potential.
💡 PRO TIP: The Content Gap Exploit
VidIQ Competition Score under 50 = low competition. Find keywords where Article traffic is > 5K/mo BUT top YouTube videos have < 100K views. This is a HUGE content gap.
