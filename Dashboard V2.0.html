Uncover high-intent keywords your competitors are monetizing. This validates profitability using CPC, ensures growing demand via trend data, and pinpoints content gaps where low YouTube competition meets strong article traffic.

Sweet spot filter
1K–50K traffic + intent = Commercial (C) or Informational (I). These pages rank well enough to validate the niche but lack the authority to defend against superior content.
The CPC predictor
CPC above $1.50 = high buyer intent. CPC above $3.00 = lucrative niche with strong YouTube RPM & affiliate conversion potential.
The content gap exploit
VidIQ competition under 50 = low competition. Find keywords where article traffic is > 5K/mo BUT top YouTube videos have < 100K views. That's a massive opening.
