❓ FAQ Schema Items
Q: What size air fryer do I need for a family of 4?
A: For a family of 4, a 5–6 quart air fryer is ideal. It can cook a whole chicken or 4–5 servings of fries at once without overcrowding.
Q: Are air fryers healthier than deep frying?
A: Yes — air fryers use 70–80% less oil, significantly reducing calorie and fat content while achieving similar crispiness.
Q: How long do air fryers last?
A: A quality air fryer lasts 3–5 years with regular use. Brands like Ninja and COSORI typically offer 1-year warranties.
Q: Can you put foil in an air fryer?
A: Yes, you can use aluminium foil, but never cover the basket completely — airflow is essential for cooking.
