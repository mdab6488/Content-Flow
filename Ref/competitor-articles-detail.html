keyword details analysis

Enter a keyword to see which competitor pages rank for it — along with traffic estimates, search intent, and content signals to guide your strategy.

Tips & Filters
Filter by Language: Set to English (or your target language).
Filter by Organic Traffic: Set the minimum traffic to 500 or 1,000 to only see articles that are actually getting consistent views.
Filter by Word Count: If you want to see long-form guides, set the minimum word count to 1,500.
Look for: The "Search Intent" label (Informational, Navigational, Commercial, Transactional) tells you exactly what kind of article you need to write.
PRO TIP: The Freshness Exploit
Check the 'Last Updated' date. If the competitor's article is 6+ months old, they're missing newer product releases. Feature the newer models in your video/article for instant relevance and higher CTR.
PRO TIP: GEO-Ready Content
PAA questions = pre-built video chapters AND pre-built FAQ schema. Answer each one clearly in your content for instant GEO-ready structured data.
